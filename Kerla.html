About Kerla

Kerla is a beutiful state in the southwestern part of india, along the arabian sea.Known as Gods Own Contry, it is famous for its lush green landscapes,peaceful backwaters,rich traditions,and ayurvedic practices. Its one of the most popular tourist destination is india for both nature lovers and culture entusiasts
