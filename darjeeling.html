About Darjeeling

Darjeeling in West Bengal is a scenic hill station known for its tea gardens, toy train, and views of Kanchenjunga the world’s third-highest mountain.

Culture and Tradition

  • Mixed culture of Nepali, Tibetan, Bengali, and British colonial influence
  • Popular festivals include Losar (Tibetan New Year) and Tihar

Famous Dishes

  • Darjeeling Momos
  • Thukpa
  • Aloo Dum
  • Darjeeling Tea

Best Time to Visit

  • March to May Pleasant weather, blooming flowers, and clear mountain views
  • October to November Crisp air and excellent views of Kanchenjunga

Things To do

  • Ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Visit Tiger Hill for sunrise view of Kanchenjunga
  • Explore tea estates and Peace Pagoda

Budget (Per Day)

  • Budget: ₹1000–₹1500
  • Mid-range: ₹2000–₹3000
  • Luxury: ₹4000+
